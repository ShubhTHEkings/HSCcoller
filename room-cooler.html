Frequently Asked Questions

How much area can this cooler effectively cool?

This Room-cooler can effectively cool areas up to 400 square feet (approximately 37 square meters). For larger spaces, you may need multiple coolers or consider our industrial models.

How often do I need to refill the water tank?

With its 45-liter capacity, the water tank typically needs to be refilled once or twice daily during peak summer. This depends on factors like humidity, temperature, and usage duration. The cooler comes with a convenient water level indicator to help you monitor the water level.

Is this cooler energy efficient?

Yes, this cooler is highly energy efficient. It consumes only 170 watts of electricity, which is approximately 80-85% less than a typical air conditioner. This translates to approximately ₹2 per hour of operation based on average electricity rates.

How loud is this cooler during operation?

At low and medium speeds, the noise level is quite manageable (approximately 45-55 dB), similar to a normal conversation. At high speed, it's around 65 dB, which is noticeable but still comfortable for most environments. For bedroom use, we recommend the low or medium settings.

Does this cooler work well in humid conditions?

Desert coolers work best in dry conditions with low humidity. In areas with humidity above 60%, the cooling efficiency may be reduced. Our cooler incorporates specialized high-density cooling pads that provide decent cooling even in moderately humid conditions, but for very humid regions, we recommend our room cooler models instead.

How long is the warranty period?

This cooler comes with a 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects. We also offer extended warranty options at an additional cost. The motor, which is the most critical component, is covered for the full warranty period.

Do you offer installation services?

The cooler is designed for easy setup and doesn't require professional installation. It comes fully assembled with castor wheels for mobility. Simply fill the water tank, plug it in, and it's ready to use. However, we do offer basic setup assistance in select cities at an additional charge of ₹200.

How do I maintain this cooler?

For optimal performance, we recommend cleaning the water tank and replacing the water every 2-3 days. The cooling pads should be cleaned monthly during the usage season. Before long-term storage, drain all water, clean the tank and pads thoroughly, and store in a dry place. The user manual included with the product provides detailed maintenance guidelines.
